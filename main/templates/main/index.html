About Sattva Sponsor

Since its inception in 2011, Sattva has only grown as a fest by amalgamating more genres, having bigger, better events and amassing larger footfalls every single year. This 10th edition of Sattva is no different. As we keep in mind the pandemic and social distancing norms, our team has relentlessly worked towards making this edition of Sattva possible as we transition into a fully online fest.

Sattva 21 will be conducted in two phases. The first phase will take place between January 4 and January 8, 2021. Participants are required to register latest by 23rd December and send in their videos for the online screening by 30th December.

During the first phase, the judges will go through the all of submissions done by the participants and select the few best ones to move on. Results of phase 1 will be announced on 10th January, 2021. The selected participants will move on to phase two after which the winners for each event will be announced. The dates for phase 2 will be announced shortly on our website as well as our Instagram page. To know more about all the events, check out the events tab on our website. Through Sattva 21, we hope to alleviate some of the inconveniences caused during the pandemic and bounce back stronger from a year like 2020.

Cyclothon is the precursor to Sattva and through this enthralling affair we hope to offer a glimpse of the upcoming festivities that Sattva has to offer. It oversees 1500+ cyclists battling it out in the wee hours of a Sunday and is generally held in September and is comfortably one of the biggest cyclothons organised in Mumbai. It is organised in such a way that its route is one of the most happening areas of suburban Mumbai so as to catch maximum eyeballs. Banners of our sponsors are displayed across suburban Mumbai in addition to relentless publicity and the numerous standees and posters that are put up in almost all colleges in and around Mumbai.

This year, however, due to the unfortunate circumstances of the COVID-19 pandemic, Team Sattva was unable to pull of this exquisite pre-event of the fest. However, we do plan on incorporating other means to bring in the Sattva fever amongst Mumbaikars.
